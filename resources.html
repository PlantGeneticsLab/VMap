VMap1

A genus-level genetic variation map of wheat (VMap 1.0) performed whole-genome sequencing of 414 accessions from 25 subspecies of wheat relatives in the genus Triticum and Aegilops.

VMap2

Wheat Whole-genome Genetic Variation Map 2.0 (VMap 2.0) originally was planned in 2018 whose goal was the complete mapping from diploid Aegilops tauschii to tetraploid and hexaploid bread wheat, as well as understanding the natural selection and domestication, particularly removal of deleterious mutations.
